Metal music

What's metal music?

The metal music genre is characterized by distorted guitar and bass playing, loud drumming and aggressive vocals. Metal was created because of a workplace accident; Tony Iommi, the legendary Black Sabbath guitarist, sliced the tops of his fingers on his right hand in a factory.
